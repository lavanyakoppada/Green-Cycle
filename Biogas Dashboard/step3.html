STEP 3:  PREPARE THE DIGESTER

The biogas digester tank must be clean of any leftover residue or debris for a successful start of the digestion cycle. 

Also, inspect all the components, like valves and pipes. Double-check that everything is connected correctly and in 
good working order. 
    
Last but not least, confirm that the digester is airtight. Only a good seal can maintain the right conditions for the process.
